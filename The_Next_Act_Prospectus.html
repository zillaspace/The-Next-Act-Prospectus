Campaign Positioning

Preserve Aspen's Cultural Heart

The ISIS Theatre deserves a renovation to be celebrated as the premiere cultural landmark it has the potential to be, not only as a building with naming opportunities. For affluent donors, the emotional purchase is legacy. The financial gift becomes a permanent place in the Aspen story.

The powerful message is not only help us renovate, but help us write the next act. Naming rights turn patrons into producers of Aspen's future.

The Next Act

A campaign to renovate the Isis with the glamour of a premiere, the seriousness of preservation, the intimacy of a private Aspen salon, and the importance of community accessibility.

Donor Recognition

The Aspen Story Wall

Instead of building a flat donor wall, we will build an elegant story installation.

Each major donor can have a refined bronze or stone plaque with a QR story page, a personal Aspen memory, a portrait, a quote, and connection to film, culture, or community. It becomes part museum, part private club, part civic archive.

This gives donors something far more valuable than their name in a list. It gives them context.
